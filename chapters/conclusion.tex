\chapter*{Conclusion}

The main goal of this tutorial is to present the capabilities that modern Android mobile devices can offer for performing scientific computing in a distributed environment\index{distributed computing}. Without pretending to be comprehensive, the presented material aims to provoke the reader's creative thinking and inspire him to create his own author's projects with the examined technologies and the affected scientific fields.

The authors are grateful to their readers for their time and attention and warmly encourage the submission of feedback and the sharing of exciting thoughts, ideas, or suggestions to the contacts indicated in the link.
